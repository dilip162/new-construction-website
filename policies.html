DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION

At London Building Company we are keen to attract and retain talented and enthusiastic people, those who can prove that they work on their own initiative in a progressive and challenging environment, and those with the right skills and experience to leverage the success and legacy of our thriving company.

London Building Company is committed to equal pay and to providing fulfilling and enriching careers for our people – regardless of gender, religion, sexual orientation or disability.
