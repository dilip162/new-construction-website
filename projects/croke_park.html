Projects Fitout Croke Park

Croke Park

Fitout - Residential

A contemporary development of nine apartments over four floors in a single building in the London Borough of Southwark. A blend of six, 2 bedroom and three, 3 bedroom apartments each with en suite facilities together with 2,000 sqft of commercial space at ground floor. The secure entrance leads to lobby areas with a lift serving all floors. All apartments benefit from private balconies and share communal amenity space and roof garden area. Amongst breweries and tanneries, now predominantly private dwellings, the development is less than a mile from Tower Bridge and London Bridge Station.
